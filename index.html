Welcome to the Lab Website for Stat 311

This website contains the materials for Stat 311 at the University of Washington. The labs were first adopted from OpenIntro for the Autumn 2019 version of Stat 311, and have been updated in Summer 2020. Comments, suggestions and contributions are welcome: send an email to grover4@uw.edu.
